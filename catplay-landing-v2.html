What CatPlay is

Not a tracker. A ritual.

Three quiet things CatPlay does well, and a hundred things it deliberately doesn't.

01
A daily play prompt

One nudge at the hour your cat is actually awake and alert. No streak shame, no buzzing all day. Just a quiet reminder that the moment is here.

The daily loop
02
A streak that means something

Honest streaks. If you miss a day, CatPlay doesn't punish you. It notes it, and welcomes you back without drama. Consistency, not perfection.

Emotional retention
03
A weekly Report Card

Every Sunday: a small, beautiful summary. Time played, days you showed up, what your cat seems to prefer. Shareable, keepable, yours.

Growth engine
How it works

Three minutes to set up.

A tiny onboarding, a daily moment, a quiet reflection at the end of the week.

1
Tell us about your cat
Name, age, indoor or outdoor, and a couple of quick questions about energy and mood. Takes a minute. CatPlay uses it to pick the right time and the right kind of play.
2
Show up for the ritual
CatPlay nudges you once a day, at the hour your cat is most likely to engage. Five minutes of focused play is all it asks for.
3
Watch the picture deepen
Each session adds a quiet observation to your cat's profile. Over weeks, you start to see them more clearly than you ever have.

Questions

The honest FAQ.

If you're wondering it, you're probably not alone.

When does CatPlay launch? +
CatPlay is in active development for both iOS and Android, targeting 2026. The waitlist is the best way to hear first and to be among the early users who shape what the app becomes.
Is CatPlay free? +
CatPlay is a paid app from day one. A subscription keeps it ad-free, sustainable, and focused entirely on quality. No free tier, no distractions, no data selling. You get the full experience from the start.
Will it work on Android? +
Yes. CatPlay is being built for both iOS and Android from the beginning. Both platforms will launch together.
Do I need to track everything manually? +
No. The whole point of CatPlay is that it disappears into the background. One tap to start a session, one tap to end it. The app does the noticing for you.
Will my data stay private? +
Yes. CatPlay stores your cat's profile and session history privately and never sells data. No ads means no reason to track you. The full privacy policy is linked in the footer.
Who is behind CatPlay? +
CatPlay is built by Pascal Misoph, an independent developer in Dresden, Germany. One person, one app, built for cat owners who pay attention. More in the About section below.
About

Made with care.

An independent project, made for cat owners who'd rather notice than scroll.

P

CatPlay started with Zula and Nali, my two cats, and the realization that after a long, busy workday, I kept defaulting to the same routine: food, litter, a quick pat on the head. Days would pass without real, focused attention. They deserved better. I just needed something to hold me accountable.

I looked for an app that could do that. Something small, something warm. Not a vet tracker, not a social network for pets, not a game with XP points. Just a quiet nudge to show up for five minutes and actually play. It didn't exist. So I started building it.

CatPlay is being built independently, in evenings and weekends, by someone who knows what it's like to love your cats deeply and still let the days slip by on autopilot.

Pascal, founder (and staff to Zula & Nali)
