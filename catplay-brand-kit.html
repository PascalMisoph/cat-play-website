05 — Photography Direction

Lived-in. Warm. Honest.

Cats in real homes — soft afternoon light, a windowsill, a tangled blanket. Never studio. Never staged. Never the same five stock cats with green eyes against a white wall.

Window light
Hands & toy
Dusk · Hunt
Resting close
Eye contact
Texture · Fur
✓ Look for
  • Natural side or window light, golden hour
  • The cat in their actual home, not a set
  • Owner present in frame — a hand, a knee
  • Movement: hunt, leap, mid-stretch
  • Texture: fur, blanket, wood, ceramic bowl

A cat mid-pounce on a wooden floor at 7pm, low light, slight motion blur.

✗ Avoid
  • White-background studio cat photography
  • Fisheye / wide-angle "funny cat face" shots
  • Cats in costumes, hats, or props
  • Overprocessed HDR, oversaturated colors
  • The same five Pinterest stock cats

Glamour-shot tabby on white seamless, pin-sharp, retouched whiskers.

06 — Voice & Tone

How CatPlay speaks.

Calm. Specific. Never gamified, never guilt-driven. The app is a quiet companion — it notices, it remembers, it doesn't push.

✓ We sound like
  • A trusted friend, not a coach
  • Specific over generic ("at dusk" not "later")
  • Warm without being saccharine
  • Confident in small moments
  • Honest when a day is missed

"You missed yesterday. Mochi will still be here tonight."

✗ We avoid
  • "Furbabies", "purr-fect", cat puns
  • Streak shaming or guilt language
  • Excessive emojis 🐾🎉🔥
  • Gamification words: XP, level up, unlock
  • Hype tone: "AMAZING!", "Crush your goals!"

"🔥 Don't break your streak! Level up your purr-fect cat parent score! 🐾"

07 — Motion Principles

Movement that arrives, not performs.

Animation in CatPlay should feel like breath — slow enough to notice, soft enough to disappear. Never engagement bait.

Arrive
320ms · ease-out
Cards and surfaces fade and rise gently into place. No bounce, no spring. Just settle.
Confirm
240ms · ease-in-out
Streak tick, session complete — a soft glow from amber, not a celebration burst.
Progress
600ms · cubic-bezier
A bar fills slowly enough to feel earned. Time itself is part of the ritual.
Press
120ms · ease-out
A warm, immediate response to touch. The amber deepens by one shade.
Transition
280ms · ease-in-out
Screen-to-screen flows are quiet crossfades and slides. Never a hard cut.
Idle
2.4s · loop
A near-imperceptible breathing pulse on the play card. The app is waiting, calmly.
